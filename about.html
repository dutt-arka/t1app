About

Microlearning deals with relatively small learning units and short-term learning activities.
The term is used in e-learning and related fields in the sense of learning processes in mediated environments.
It involves short-term-focused strategies especially designed for skill based understanding/learning/education.

According to a study by Microsoft, the average human being now has an attention span of eight seconds. This is a sharp decrease from the average attention span of 12 seconds in the year 2000.

This raises the need for microlearning apps even more!
